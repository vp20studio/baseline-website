How It Works

Three steps to
transformation

01

Choose Your Challenge

Pick a pre-built challenge that matches your goals, or create a custom challenge with your own rules. Select 30, 60, or 90 days based on your commitment level.

02

Sign Your Commitment

Make it official by signing a personal commitment contract with your finger. This is your promise to yourself—a line in the sand that marks the beginning.

03

Complete Daily Rules

Each day, check off your rules as you complete them. Build momentum, maintain your streak, and watch your baseline rise over time.
